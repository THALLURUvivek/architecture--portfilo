Our Story

Founded in 2010, Stackly Architecture is a collective of visionary architects, designers, and planners dedicated to creating meaningful built environments. With over 500+ projects across residential, commercial, and public sectors, we blend innovation with timeless design.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that great architecture emerges from deep listening, creative exploration, and technical excellence. Every project tells a unique story.

From humble beginnings with a team of three, we have grown into a full-service architecture firm with 40+ talented professionals. Our portfolio spans luxury residences, corporate headquarters, public institutions, and urban master plans across India and Southeast Asia.
